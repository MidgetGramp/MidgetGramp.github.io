Technology Student Association

The Technology Student Association is a STEM based program that focuses on developing leaders. It is organized on three levels: locally, on the state level, and nationally.

Belton High School TSA

The TSA at Belton High School is a chapter that meets in the Belton Yeokum Technology Center weekly to work on TSA related events. There are currently seventeen individuals in Belton High School TSA. There are six officers:

  • Michael Dickerson - President

  • Brennan Hutchison - Vice President

  • Hannah Engler - Secretary

  • Rowan Putman - Seargant at Arms

  • Trevor Galvez - Reporter

  • Michael Thompson - Treasurer

Missouri TSA

The TSA in Missouri is a chapter that meets for an annual convention in Rolla, Missouri. There are currently around three thousand five hundred individuals in Missouri TSA.
