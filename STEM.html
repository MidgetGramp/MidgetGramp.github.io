Science

One of the key portions of our school's curriculum is science. Science is an important part of how many see the world today. Belton High School strives to teach science so that the next generation will readily be able to measure and observe the real world, collect data by various means, and draw logical conclusions from said data. Belton has numerous classes that focus on the science portion of STEM:

  • AP Physics - An advanced placement class that focuses on advanced problem solving in relation to physics. Touching lightly on calculus based physics, this class incorporates numerous experimental labs to reinforce the theoretical work.
    Link to Collegeboard AP Physics 1

  • AP Botany - An advanced placement class that focuses on the micro and macro functionings of botanical organisms. Heavily based in chemistry, this class integrates live plant samples and advanced scientific equipment to aid students in identifying organisms, understanding the functions, and grasping the ecological interactions.
    Link to Collegeboard AP Physics 1

  • AP Chemistry - An advanced placement class that focuses on the chemical properties and reactions of elements and compounds, both synthetic and natural. Theoretical work is cemented with additional data from chemical labs, often involving complex tasks in order to create, identify, or react certain compounds.
    Link to Collegeboard AP Physics 1
